# Beta Access

{% hint style="warning" %}
The Beta is currently gated by a password. We are rolling out access to users in weekly cohorts. Please [sign up through our website](http://alphix.fi/) to be part of the next one.
{% endhint %}

The Beta helps us test and improve our AMM platform before the Mainnet launch. Please share your feedback with us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/NTXRarFbTr), we appreciate all input.

Setting up your wallet:

1. **Your Wallet** - Use an EVM-compatible wallet like [Rabby](https://rabby.io/)
2. **Add Network** - Connect to [Base Sepolia](https://docs.base.org/base-chain/quickstart/connecting-to-base#base-testnet-sepolia)
3. **Get ETH** - Claim free test tokens from a [Faucet](https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/base-sepolia)
4. **Alphix Faucet** - Once you have testnet ETH you can claim our Faucet to get test tokens

You're all set. [Explore Alphix](https://www.alphix.fi/swap) and help us build the future of AMMs.
